about me

I consider myself a problem solver. Presently, I like to solve problems with code and the potential of the web. I keep most of my projects on GitHub, so head over to browse. You can see some of my experience on Zerply and I use Twitter to publish my writing and interact, so reach out and show me something interesting.

I studied Math and Political Economy at Bennington College where I picked up some video production and story telling skills, as well as extensive student governence experience. I spent most of my extracurricular time performing statistical analysis for Mirka Prazak, an Anthropology Professor at Bennington. Together we studied the effects of AIDS orphans on villages in rural Kenya.


I am open to projects, requests, and collaborations and the best way to reach me is via email or twitter.
